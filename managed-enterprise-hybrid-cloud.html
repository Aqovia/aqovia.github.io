Our Dedicated Expert Team

Aqovia’s Managed Enterprise Hybrid Cloud package deploys a team of hybrid cloud specialists who are dedicated to achieving a resilient, cost-efficient, and fully managed hybrid cloud environment for your organisation. Over 180 days, these experts work closely with your team to design, implement, and optimise a hybrid cloud solution tailored to your specific business and technical needs.

Cloud Lead:

Responsible for aligning the hybrid cloud deployment with your business goals. The Cloud Lead oversees all phases of the project, ensuring a strategic approach to integrating on-premise and cloud environments while meeting timelines and KPIs.

Cloud Architect:

Designs an adaptable and scalable architecture that connects your existing on-premise resources with cloud solutions. The Cloud Architect focuses on ensuring security, optimising performance, and supporting long-term scalability. They also evaluate and incorporate partner-enabled solutions (Metal, HPE GL, VMware, Pure) for tailored edge computing and private cloud needs.

Cloud Analyst:

Performs an in-depth analysis of current workloads, resource utilisation, and cost structures. The Cloud Analyst generates detailed reports to guide cost optimisation, network performance improvements, and efficiency gains, providing ongoing data insights for continuous improvement.

Advanced Tools for Planning, Execution, and Continuous Optimisation

Our Managed Enterprise Hybrid Cloud package is supported by advanced tools that enhance transparency, streamline planning, and maximise the effectiveness of hybrid cloud operations. These tools are designed to give you a clear view of costs, ROI, and network performance to make data-driven decisions throughout the cloud transformation journey.

Planning and Architecture Design Tool:

This tool enables precise design of a secure and robust hybrid cloud architecture. It allows our team to model different integration scenarios, ensuring compatibility and resilience between on-premises and cloud resources. With this tool, we create a tailored hybrid structure that prioritises both security and performance to meet your unique operational demands.

Cloud Costing & ROI Tool:

Budget control is key to cloud success. Our costing and ROI tool provides detailed insights into projected expenses across various cloud providers, enabling comparison of cost models and helping identify cost-saving opportunities. The tool’s total cost of ownership (TCO) calculator ensures you understand both immediate and long-term financial impacts of the hybrid setup.

Partner Tools & APIs:

Through strategic partner tools like Metal, HPE GL, VMware, and Pure, we deliver powerful edge computing and private cloud options that extend your on-prem capabilities while seamlessly integrating with public clouds. These APIs allow rapid scaling, advanced network optimisation, and secure data exchange across environments, maximising flexibility while maintaining compliance.
