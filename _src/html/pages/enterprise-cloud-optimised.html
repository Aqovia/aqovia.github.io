Our Dedicated Expert Team

Aqovia's Enterprise Cloud Optimised service brings together a team of cloud specialists, each with a specific role in delivering a streamlined, multi-cloud solution designed for efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Over a period of 60-90 days, our dedicated team members work collaboratively to assess, plan, and implement customised optimisation strategies that are fully aligned with your business objectives.

Cloud Lead:

The Cloud Lead oversees the project from start to finish, ensuring all initiatives align with your business goals and maximise the value of your cloud investments. They coordinate across teams, managing resources, timelines, and deliverables to keep the project on track and focused on delivering measurable outcomes.

Cloud Architect:

The Cloud Architect is responsible for designing an optimised, multi-cloud architecture tailored to your organisation’s specific technical requirements. Leveraging deep expertise in cloud design, they evaluate existing infrastructure and recommend architectural enhancements to boost performance, security, and scalability across all cloud environments.

Cloud Analyst:

The Cloud Analyst conducts a thorough assessment of your current cloud setup, analysing workload performance, cost drivers, and resource utilisation. They produce detailed reports on cost and process optimisation, offering actionable insights that support immediate savings and longer-term efficiency improvements. This role also involves ongoing monitoring and adjustment recommendations based on real-time cloud performance data.

Advanced Tools for Precision and Transparency​

Aqovia provides a suite of advanced tools that enable precise planning, robust architecture design, and transparent costing, helping your team navigate complex multi-cloud environments with ease.

Planning and Architecture Design Tool:

This tool is essential for creating a clear, future-proof multi-cloud architecture. It enables our team to visualise infrastructure across multiple cloud providers, identify areas for optimisation, and model potential configurations. By simulating different architecture scenarios, the tool ensures the design we recommend aligns with both technical requirements and business priorities, enhancing efficiency while minimising risks.

Cloud Costing & ROI Tool:

Cost management is central to cloud optimisation, and this tool offers detailed insights into current and projected cloud expenses. By factoring in various cost models and ROI projections, it provides an accurate comparison of different cloud providers and services, helping clients make informed choices for optimal savings. Additionally, it calculates total cost of ownership (TCO) and predicts cost savings, making the financial benefits of multi-cloud optimisation clear and quantifiable.
